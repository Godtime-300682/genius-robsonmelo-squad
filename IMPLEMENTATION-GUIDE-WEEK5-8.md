## THE CALL STRUCTURE (90 min)

### PHASE 1: PATTERN EXTRACTION (30 min)

**Your opening (2 min):**
```
"Thanks for being here. This next 90 minutes is about understanding the pattern
that makes you valuable. By the end, you'll know:

1. What your invisible method actually is
2. What to call it
3. What it's worth

Sound good? Let's go."
```

**Question 1 - Best Client Story (10 min):**
```
"Walk me through one of your best client engagements.

Start with: How did you meet?
Then: What did you do?
End with: What changed for them?"

[LISTEN. Don't interrupt. Take notes.]

LISTEN FOR:
- The process steps (how you work)
- The transformation (what changed)
- The timeline (how long)
- The speed (did you solve it faster than they expected)
- The client feedback ("You're so good at...")
```

**Question 2 - Pattern Recognition (8 min):**
```
"Great story. Here's what I'm hearing:

[Summarize what they told you in 2-3 sentences]

Is that right?"

[Wait for confirmation]

"Has this pattern happened before? Another client
getting similar results from similar work?"

[Listen for: YES = it's repeatable. NO = maybe luck.]
```

**Question 3 - Visibility (7 min):**
```
"If I asked your best 5 clients to describe how you work,
what would they say?"

[Listen. Do they have a clear answer? Or are they guessing?]

"Do they all say the same thing? Or do they describe it differently?"

[If same = the pattern is visible. If different = invisible.]
```

**Question 4 - Clarity (3 min):**
```
"How would YOU describe what you do?"

[Listen. Is it clear? Specific? Or generic?]
```

---

**Your work (10 min):**
```

[Summarize the 3-5 step process they described]

Let me show you as a framework..."

[Draw or describe a simple 3-part model]

EXAMPLE MODELS:

Model 1: Extract Problem ‚Üí Redesign Solution ‚Üí Implement Change
Model 2: Diagnose Pattern ‚Üí Clarify Position ‚Üí Execute Strategy
Model 3: Identify Blocks ‚Üí Envision Outcome ‚Üí Design System

[Describe which model matches their work]

**Their feedback (10 min):**
```

If NEVER THOUGHT: Perfect, they're seeing their method for first time.

[Adjust model based on feedback]

### STEP 3: QUANTIFY VALUE (30 min)

**Your math (show them):**

```
FORMULA: [Annual impact] √∑ [Years to achieve] = Annual value

EXAMPLE CALCULATION:

Revenue increase:
- Current: R$50K/month = R$600K/year
- Goal: R$100K/month = R$1,200K/year
- Delta: R$600K/year increase

Time savings:
- Current: 60 hours/week
- Goal: 25 hours/week
- Savings: 35 hours/week √ó R$500/hr rate = R$17.5K/week
- Annual: R$17.5K √ó 52 weeks = R$910K/year

TOTAL VALUE CREATED:
R$600K (revenue) + R$910K (time) = R$1,510K/year
```

**Show 10:1 ROI rule:**
```
"So if you're creating R$1.51M in value,
the 10:1 ROI standard would put the investment at R$151K.

But we're not charging that.

We're charging R$25-30K.

That's a 50-60:1 ROI for you.

The value gap - the difference between what we charge
and what we could charge - is R$120K+ that goes to you.

That's the deal: You get almost all the upside."
```

**Get their reaction:**
```
"Does that math make sense?"

[Wait for response]

"Does it feel worth the investment?"

[They'll likely say: "Absolutely yes" or "Let me think"]
```
