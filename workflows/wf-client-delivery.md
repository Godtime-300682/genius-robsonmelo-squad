# Workflow: Client Delivery
## Deliver Transformation to Paying Client

**Workflow ID:** WF-GR-003
**Type:** Service delivery
**Duration:** 60-90 days per client
**Complexity:** High (YOUR genius zone execution)
**Owner:** Robson Melo (with operations support)

## Overview

This workflow delivers the transformation promised in your Grand Slam Offer to a paying client (R$15K-R$30K). It ensures consistent, high-quality delivery while documenting for future systemization.

**Input:** Signed contract + client payment
**Output:** Transformation delivered + case study + testimonial + potential upsell

**Phases:** 5
**Agents involved:** 6
**Quality checkpoints:** 5

## PHASE 1: ONBOARDING (Days 1-3)
**Goal:** Client feels confident, process is clear, delivery begins smoothly

**Primary Agent:** @sam-carpenter (systematization)
**Supporting:** @michael-gerber (operations)

### Step 1.1: Welcome Package (Day 1)
**Task:** Send within 24 hours of contract signing

**Email content:**
- Welcome message (congratulations on decision)
- What happens next (timeline overview)
- Onboarding form link (Google Form/Typeform)
- Access credentials (if using portal/Slack/etc.)
- Calendar link for kickoff call (within 48h)

**Onboarding form fields:**
- Company overview
- Current situation (baseline)
- Desired outcome (specific)
- KPIs they care about
- Team members involved
- Obstacles anticipated
- Success definition

**Deliverable:** Welcome email sent + form completed

### Step 1.2: Kickoff Call (Days 2-3)
**Task:** 60-90 minute deep dive

**Agenda:**
1. **Relationship building** (5 min)
   - Personal connection
   - Thank them for trust

2. **Objectives review** (15 min)
   - Confirm desired outcome
   - Validate KPIs from proposal
   - Align on success metrics

3. **Process walkthrough** (20 min)
   - Explain your method (high-level)
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Roles (what you do, what they do)

4. **Discovery questions** (30 min)
   - Apply probative questions IN THEIR CONTEXT
   - Begin pattern identification
   - Listen for invisible patterns

5. **Next steps** (10 min)
   - Schedule next session
   - Homework/prep for them
   - Confirm communication channels

**Deliverable:** Kickoff call completed + detailed notes

### Step 1.3: Delivery Kickoff (Day 3)
**Task:** Activate delivery system

**Process:**
1. Create client folder (shared drive, Notion, etc.)
2. Set up communication channel (Slack, email, WhatsApp)
3. Schedule recurring check-ins (weekly recommended)
4. Activate project tracking (Trello, Asana, or simple spreadsheet)
5. Send "Next Steps" recap email

**Deliverable:** Delivery infrastructure active

**Checkpoint (Day 3):**
- [ ] Welcome package sent + completed ‚úì
- [ ] Kickoff call completed ‚úì
- [ ] Delivery infrastructure active ‚úì
- [ ] Client feels confident ‚úì

## PHASE 2: DISCOVERY (Days 4-14)
**Goal:** Deep understanding of THEIR situation, identify invisible pattern in THEIR context

**Primary Agent:** @david-c-baker (pattern-matching)
**Supporting:** Robson Melo (YOUR genius zone)

### Step 2.1: Context Deep Dive (Days 4-7)
**Task:** Investigate their situation using probative questions

**Activities:**
- **Sessions:** 2-3 deep dive sessions (90 min each)
- **Method:** Apply your probative questions
- **Goal:** Understand their context deeply enough to see the invisible pattern

**Questions framework:**
1. **Situation questions**
   - "Walk me through how [PROCESS] currently works"
   - "What does a typical [SCENARIO] look like?"
   - "Who's involved at each stage?"

2. **Problem questions**
   - "Where does it break down?"
   - "What frustrates you most?"
   - "What have you tried already?"

3. **Pattern questions** (YOUR genius)
   - "What do you think is causing this?"
   - "How long has this been happening?"
   - "Does it happen in other areas too?"

**Deliverable:** Discovery notes + initial pattern hypothesis

### Step 2.2: Pattern Diagnosis (Days 8-10)
**Task:** Identify the invisible pattern in THEIR specific context

**Process:**
1. **Review discovery notes**
   - What's the recurring theme?
   - What are they missing?
   - How does YOUR pattern apply to THEM?

2. **Formulate diagnosis**
   - "Here's what I'm seeing that you might not..."
   - Connect to your method framework
   - Articulate the invisible pattern

3. **Validate with client**
   - Present diagnosis
   - Get "aha moment" confirmation
   - Adjust if needed

**Deliverable:** Pattern diagnosis document (1-2 pages)

### Step 2.3: Baseline Documentation (Days 11-14)
**Task:** Document current state (before transformation)

**Metrics to capture:**
- Quantitative (numbers, KPIs)
- Qualitative (pain points, frustrations)
- Process maps (how things work now)
- Key players (who's involved)

**Purpose:** Before/after comparison for case study

**Deliverable:** Baseline document

**Checkpoint (Day 14):**
- [ ] 2-3 deep dive sessions completed ‚úì
- [ ] Invisible pattern identified in their context ‚úì
- [ ] Client had "aha moment" ‚úì
- [ ] Baseline documented ‚úì

## PHASE 3: TRANSFORMATION (Days 15-50)
**Goal:** Apply your method to transform their situation

**Primary Agent:** Robson Melo (YOUR GENIUS ZONE - do NOT delegate this)
**Supporting:** @sam-carpenter (document process), @gino-wickman (track progress)

### Step 3.1: Transformation Plan (Days 15-17)
**Task:** Map your method to their situation

**Process:**
1. **Review your method framework** (from Phase 1 of creation)
   - Your 3-7 steps
   - Decision points
   - Key activities

2. **Customize for their context**
   - Step 1 becomes: [SPECIFIC ACTION IN THEIR CONTEXT]
   - Step 2 becomes: [SPECIFIC ACTION IN THEIR CONTEXT]
   - etc.

3. **Create transformation roadmap**
   - Milestones (what gets achieved when)
   - Quick wins (early results to build momentum)
   - Major transformations (end results)

**Deliverable:** Transformation plan (3-5 pages)

### Step 3.2: Implementation Phase (Days 18-45)
**Task:** Execute transformation with client

**Weekly structure:**
- **Monday:** Progress check-in (30 min)
- **Mid-week:** Deep work session (90 min)
- **Friday:** Results review + next week planning (30 min)

**Per session:**
1. **Review progress** (what's done)
2. **Address obstacles** (what's blocking)
3. **Apply next step of method** (your framework)
4. **Assign homework** (what they implement between sessions)
5. **Track results** (measure against KPIs)

**Critical:**
- THIS is your Genius Zone - pattern application
- Document EVERYTHING (for future systemization)
- Note: What can be systematized vs. what requires YOUR genius

**Deliverable:** Weekly session notes (4-6 weeks √ó 3 sessions = 12-18 sessions)

### Step 3.3: Mid-Point Review (Day 30)
**Task:** Formal check-in with client

**Agenda:**
1. **Progress review**
   - What's changed so far?
   - What results are appearing?
   - How do they feel?

2. **Metrics check**
   - Measure against baseline
   - Quantify improvements
   - Identify gaps

3. **Course correction** (if needed)
   - What's working?
   - What needs adjustment?
   - Pivot if necessary

4. **Recommitment**
   - Confirm we're on track
   - Renewed energy for second half

**Deliverable:** Mid-point review document

### Step 3.4: Final Push (Days 31-50)
**Task:** Complete transformation

**Focus:**
- Finish applying all steps of method
- Address remaining obstacles
- Maximize results before conclusion
- Prepare for handoff/sustainment

**Deliverable:** Transformation delivered (or very close)

**Checkpoint (Day 50):**
- [ ] Transformation plan executed ‚úì
- [ ] 12-18 sessions completed ‚úì
- [ ] Mid-point review passed ‚úì
- [ ] Results visible (quantitative + qualitative) ‚úì
- [ ] Client satisfied with progress ‚úì

**Goal:** Capture transformation for case study, testimonial, and future marketing

**Task:** Measure final results against baseline

**Deliverable:** Results summary (1-2 pages with numbers)

### Step 4.2: Case Study Creation (@donald-miller)
**Task:** Document transformation as story

**StoryBrand format:**
1. **Character:** Who is the client? (anonymize if needed)
   - Industry, role, situation

2. **Problem:** What invisible pattern were they missing?
   - External problem (tangible)
   - Internal problem (frustration)
   - Philosophical problem (injustice)

3. **Guide:** How did you (and your method) help?
   - Your expertise
   - Your method
   - Why only you could see the pattern

4. **Plan:** What process did you follow?
   - Your method steps
   - How it unfolded
   - Key moments

5. **Action:** What did they implement?
   - Specific actions taken
   - Challenges overcome

6. **Success:** What results appeared?
   - Quantitative (numbers)
   - Qualitative (how they feel)
   - Transformation visible

7. **Failure Avoided:** What would have happened without your method?
   - Cost of not acting
   - Reinforces value

**Deliverable:** Case study (3-5 pages)

### Step 4.3: Testimonial Capture (@russell-brunson)

**Process:**
1. **Prime for success**

   - "What was your situation before?"
   - "What made you decide to work with me?"
   - "What was the experience like?"
   - "What results did you achieve?"
   - "What would you say to someone considering this?"

4. **Celebrate them publicly** (@russell-brunson principle)
   - Share their win (with permission)
   - Make them the hero of the story
   - Amplify their success

**Deliverable:** Video or written testimonial + permission to use
