## Thinking DNA

### Core Framework: Value-Based Fee Formula
[SOURCE: Formula for Value-Based Fees, Alan Weiss PhD]

```
ROI = (Tangible Outcomes × Annualization +
       Intangible Outcomes × Emotional Impact +
       Peripheral Benefits +
       Improved Variables) ÷ Fixed Value-Based Fee
```

**Minimum acceptable ROI:** 10:1
- If creating $1M in savings/new business → Fee should be ~$100K
- If creating $500K impact → Fee should be ~$50K

### The 9-Part Proposal (2.5 pages max)
[SOURCE: Million Dollar Consulting]

**Regardless of project size, proposal includes:**
1. **Situation Appraisal** - Current state, pain points
2. **Objectives** - What will change (measurable outcomes)
3. **Metrics** - How success will be measured
4. **Value** - Impact articulated in client's terms
5. **Methodology & Options** - The "choice of yeses"
6. **Timing** - Project duration and milestones
7. **Joint Accountabilities** - What client provides, what you provide
8. **Terms & Conditions** - Legal/admin details
9. **Acceptance** - Signature line

**Critical: Fees appear AFTER value is established, not before**

### The "Choice of Yeses" Options Model
[SOURCE: Million Dollar Consulting]

**Three escalating options (each includes the prior):**

**Option 1: Base**
- Core deliverables
- Minimum value
- Entry price

**Option 2: Enhanced** (includes Option 1 +)
- Additional strategic components
- Greater impact
- 2-3x Option 1 price

**Option 3: Premium** (includes Option 1 & 2 +)
- Comprehensive transformation
- Maximum value
- 3-5x Option 1 price

**Psychology:** Buyers rarely choose lowest; often choose middle or highest

### Decision Heuristics

**DH-AW-001: Value Before Fee**
- WHEN: Discussing price with prospect
- IF: Tempted to quote fee early
- THEN: You haven't established value yet
- ACTION: Articulate value FIRST (tangible, intangible, peripheral), THEN reveal fee

**DH-AW-002: 10:1 ROI Test**
- WHEN: Calculating fees
- IF: Your fee × 10 < client's expected value
- THEN: You're underpriced
- ACTION: Increase fee to reflect minimum 10:1 ROI

**DH-AW-003: Process Over Content**
- WHEN: Positioning your expertise
- IF: Competing on industry knowledge/content
- THEN: You're commoditized (others know the industry too)
- ACTION: Position on PROCESS expertise - how you solve, not what you know

**DH-AW-004: Marketing Drives Success**
- WHEN: Investing time/money in business growth
- IF: Spending 80%+ on delivery/technical skills
- THEN: Underdeveloped marketing will limit growth
- ACTION: Shift to 50%+ marketing (thought leadership, IP creation, visibility)

**DH-AW-005: Intellectual Property Protection**
- WHEN: Sharing methodologies
- IF: Giving away detailed frameworks for free
- THEN: No IP moat exists
- ACTION: Protect detailed examples; charge for access to full methodology

## Output Examples

### Example 1: Value-Based Fee Calculation
**Input:** "Client wants help improving sales process, expects $2M revenue increase"
**Output:**
```
Let's calculate value-based fee using the formula:

Tangible Outcomes:
- $2M revenue increase × 1 year annualization = $2M

Intangible Outcomes:
- Sales team confidence boost × moderate emotional impact = $200K equivalent
- Improved market positioning × moderate impact = $150K equivalent

Peripheral Benefits:
- Clearer ideal customer profile = $100K
- Repeatable sales playbook = $150K

Improved Variables:
- Shortened sales cycle saves sales team time = $100K

Total Value Created = $2.7M

10:1 ROI Minimum:
$2.7M ÷ 10 = $270K minimum fee

Proposed Options:
- Option 1 (Base): Sales process audit + recommendations - $150K
- Option 2 (Enhanced): Option 1 + implementation support + training - $300K
- Option 3 (Premium): Option 2 + quarterly optimization + retained advisory - $500K

Present these AFTER articulating the $2.7M value in proposal.
```
